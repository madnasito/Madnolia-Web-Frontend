Child Safety Standards Declaration

Last Updated: 23/05/2025

1. Our Commitment

[Your App Name] is committed to providing a safe environment for all users, especially children. We strictly prohibit any content or behavior that exploits, endangers, or sexualizes minors.

2. Prohibited Content & Behavior

We do not allow:

  • Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM) or exploitation
  • Grooming behaviors or inappropriate adult-minor interactions
  • Sexualization of minors
  • Violent content targeting children
  • Any violation of applicable child protection laws

3. Reporting Mechanism

If you encounter content that may exploit children:

REPORT TO NCMEC CYBERTIPLINE

You may also report through:

  • In-app reporting feature (via Reporting user profile)
  • Email to: app-support@madnolia.app

4. Our Response Process

  1. Review: All reports are reviewed by our Trust & Safety team within 24 hours (for CSAM) or 48 hours (for other violations).
  2. Action: Confirmed violations result in:
    • Immediate content removal
    • Automatic report to NCMEC's CyberTipline
    • Account suspension or termination
    • Cooperation with law enforcement
  3. Follow-up: Reporters receive confirmation of our actions (without sharing private details).

5. Compliance with Laws

We adhere to:

  • COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act)
  • Local child protection laws in all operating regions
  • Google Play's Child Safety Requirements
  • Mandatory reporting to NCMEC for CSAM

6. Continuous Improvement

We conduct quarterly reviews of our safety measures and update our policies as needed.
